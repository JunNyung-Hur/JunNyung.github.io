JunNyung Hur received the B.S. degree in computer engineering from Kookmin University, South Korea, in 2019, where he is currently pursuing the master’s degree (advisors: MyungKeun Yoon). His current research interests include:

  • Network traffic anomaly detection using machine learning
  • Network packet analysis for data leakage prevention
  • Similarity analysis and searching technology to deal with malware bigdata environment
